About Campus Connect

Our college’s grand festival uniting technical brilliance, cultural heritage, and sporting excellence.

Campus Connect is our college’s flagship annual festival, uniting technical innovation, cultural diversity, and sporting excellence. It celebrates the spirit of our college tradition by offering students a platform to learn, perform, and compete, while fostering collaboration, creativity, and lifelong memories.

Technical

Dive into hackathons, coding challenges, robotics, and workshops that spark innovation & creativity.

Cultural

Experience vibrant dance, music, theatre, and fashion shows celebrating tradition and diversity.

Sports

From cricket and football to athletics and indoor games, enjoy the thrill of team spirit & competition.
